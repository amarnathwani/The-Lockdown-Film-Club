THE CLUB

We created a film club for those mostly at home that are passionate about film and tv. You can read more About Us if you like. Our website is a compilation of reviews, discussion, news about upcoming movies and our limited get togethers for the time being. Twice a month, we get together to watch a film in theatres that we're looking forward to. With all the measures in place, we would still like to support the films and teams we love however we can. Send us a message if you'd like to join in, or feel free to purchase tickets to our gatherings here.

You can read more about individual films on our Discussion section, and contribute your ideas, opinions and reflections on film there. If you have suggestions for films to see, give us a shout and we can look into it. If you like what you see, give us a follow on our social medias down below. Have fun, and enjoy the show!
